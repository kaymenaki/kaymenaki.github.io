Tweeble

Tweeble has been inspired by the polarisation among Twitter users on the UK EU referendum (“remainers” vs. “leavers”). However, the design and implementation of Tweeble is generic enough to support other bubbles as well in the future (e.g supporters vs. critics of Donald Trump).

The application allows visitors to explore Twitter bubbles and prominent Twitter users that contribute to them. The website collects and presents posts from Twitter users grouped into “bubbles”.

Functions

The functionality of the website supports filtering and paging tweets, visualisation (using wordclouds) of the most frequent words in tweets of particular bubbles, as well as recommending, adding and deleting users from the system.
