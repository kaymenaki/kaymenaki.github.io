Eirini Kaymenaki

UX/UI Designer based in York, UK

Born in Athens and based in York, I am passionate about designing engaging user experiences for digital media. I currently work for HSBC Holdings plc where I create rich user experiences for the public websites, internet banking and mobile (including future channels/devices) using state-of-the-art web technologies. I am also studying part-time for an MSc in Design and Digital Media at the University of Edinburgh.

Before joining HSBC, I was designing and developing mobile and web-based products and campaings for YBS Group and before that I was a proud member of the Digital Product Design team in Foster+Partners, where I worked as Digital Design Specialist and Adobe Applications Trainer. I hold a BA in Graphics Design and in a previous life I ran my own design firm in Athens.
